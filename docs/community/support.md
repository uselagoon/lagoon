# Lagoon Community Support

Lagoon community support is available via GitHub Discussions.

Discord support has been discontinued.

_Please remember that this is not to replace your current support channels - those will remain the same. This is a place to connect with other users as well as the Lagoon maintainers._

For bugs, support requests, and repository-specific questions, please open an issue in the appropriate [uselagoon repository](https://github.com/orgs/uselagoon/repositories).

For non-support questions, idea sharing, and general community conversation, please start a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/uselagoon/lagoon/discussions).

Please continue to follow our [Code of Conduct](../code-of-conduct.md) and [Participation Guidelines](participation.md).
